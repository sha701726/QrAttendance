How It Works

1

Fill Your Information Once

Enter your personal details in the form. This information is securely stored locally in your browser and only needs to be entered once.

2

QR Scanner Activates

Once your information is saved, the QR scanner automatically starts and continuously monitors for authorized QR codes.

3

Automatic Authentication

When a QR code is scanned, the system validates the 128-bit authentication string to ensure it's an authorized attendance QR.

4

Instant Submission

Upon successful validation, your stored information is automatically submitted to the attendance system with a timestamp.
